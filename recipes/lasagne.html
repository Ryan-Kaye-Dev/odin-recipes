Lasagne Verdi al Forno

Description

A recipe from my home in Emilia-Romagna, Italy. This lasagne verde made with spinach pasta cannot be beaten.

It's excellent if you want to impress some guests — or even yourself. It may be a bit time-consuming, but it's well worth the TLC you put into it.

Ingredients

For the Pasta:

  • 5 ounces spinach - rinsed, stemmed, and dried
  • ⅝ cup semolina flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour

For the Ragu:

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 carrot, diced
